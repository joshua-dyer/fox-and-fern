--- SIPS terminal command to reformat into compressed jpeg ---
sips -Z 1200 -s format jpeg -s formatOptions 80 input.jpg --out output.jpg

---
--- cwebp command for webP conversion ---
cwebp -q 75 your-new-art.jpg -o your-new-art.webp
---

Tool,Format Support,Best Used For...
sips,"JPEG, PNG, TIFF","Resizing, rotating, and basic JPEG compression."
cwebp,WebP Only,"Converting PNG/JPEG into the tiny, fast WebP format."
