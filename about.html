The Maker

Every piece of art created here at Fox and Fern begins with the rustle of the wind through the leaves. My studio doesn't have four walls; it has a horizon. Whether I am sketching on the back porch or painting in the meadow, the light and life of the natural world is my constant collaborator...

My goal is to capture the quiet, whimsical moments of nature and bring them to life through illustration and design.
